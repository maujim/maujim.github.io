Compute-Rich World Thesis

visual explainer diagram · thesis-long.txt

Scientific advantage is shifting from who has compute to who can build better systems for converting compute into trustworthy discovery: proprietary data, expert-grounded abstractions, and tight hypothesis-testing loops.

Compute is abundant Truth loops are scarce Biology = underused design space

Problem Framing

The Bottleneck Has Moved

  • Compute access alone no longer guarantees differentiated research outcomes.
  • The scarce asset is organizational ability to define and validate high-value questions.
  • Epistemic rigor and reusable knowledge infrastructure become compounding moats.

Opportunity Thesis

Biology as a Searchable Design Space

  • Nature encodes solved engineering strategies: thermal, structural, sensory, collective.
  • Current sources are fragmented across papers, collections, observations, and media.
  • A functional-trait ontology can translate biological intelligence into engineering transfer.
