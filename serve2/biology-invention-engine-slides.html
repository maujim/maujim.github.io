The Bottleneck Has Moved

Shift in Scarcity

Compute Is Necessary, Not Sufficient

  • Model scaling and hardware access are no longer the only strategic differentiators.
  • The hard part becomes selecting high-value questions and testing them rigorously.
  • Winning teams build loops that turn weak signals into durable knowledge assets.
  • Proprietary interfaces to reality outperform generic internet-scale corpora.

Core Claim

Future research advantage = epistemic discipline × expert-grounded data × fast experimental loops.

Opportunity

Biology Is an Under-Indexed Design Archive

Nature already solved flight, thermal regulation, adhesion, distributed coordination, filtration, and resilience under constraints. We have the knowledge fragments, but not an AI-native substrate for transfer.

Current Fragmentation

  • Papers and case studies (text-heavy, weakly linked)
  • Museum/field observations (siloed metadata)
  • Video/imagery (high signal, low structure)
  • Sparse functional ontologies for engineering transfer
